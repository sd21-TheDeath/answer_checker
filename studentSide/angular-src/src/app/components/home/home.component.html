About the website :

This website had been developed by the students of IT-314 group-34 of 2016 batch in the winter semester of 2018 -19 as a part of the compulsory project for Software Engineering subject. This website employs MEAN stack for both front end and back end services, along with Bootstrap for front end and python for answer checking and report generation. The website was developed by Saket, Abhishek, Tirth and Kalpit. Testing was performed by Divyesh and Rushi. The software development model used was Agile SCRUM for which documentation was taken care of by Parth and Vivek. The website was deployed on 17th of April 2019.

About DA-IICT :

DA-IICT was established in 2001. It is one of India’s leading institutes in the field of ICT engineering. Started as a research institute, it has developed into one of the most in demand institutes due to its world class research infrastructure, faculty as well as the placement it offers. As of today, DA-IICT offers degrees is B.Tech in Information and Communication Technology ( with minors in Computational Science ), M. Tech in Computer Science as well as VLSI, MSc IT, M. Des. as well as PhD. For placement companies like Amazon, Morgan Stanley, Goldman Sachs visit the campus every year. Since its inception, DA-IICT has been growing in terms of academics as well as network and is expected to achieve better and better feats every passing year.
